Privacy Policy

Effective December 27, 2025 • Updated February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Matthews Fitness ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, stores, and safeguards your information.

Matthews Fitness is intended for adults aged 18 and older and is not designed for use by children.

2. Information We Collect

To provide fitness and nutrition tracking services, Matthews Fitness may collect the following types of data:

Personal Information

  • Email address (collected via Google Sign-In)
  • A unique Account ID (also referred to as a Support ID), automatically generated to identify your account and assist with support and data deletion requests

Health & Fitness Data

  • Weight, height, date of birth, gender, and activity levels
  • Nutrition and food logs (including barcode scans, manual entries, and AI-assisted entries)
  • Workout plans, exercise history, and activity tracking
  • Water intake and daily goal progress

User Content

  • Photos of food, menus, or nutrition labels submitted for AI-based analysis
  • Images are processed temporarily to generate nutrition estimates and are not stored permanently, shared with third parties, or used to train public or shared AI models

Purchase Information

  • Subscription status for premium features (managed through Google Play Billing)
  • We do not collect or store payment details — all transactions are handled by Google Play

Device & App Information

  • Crash logs, diagnostics, and performance data used to improve app stability and reliability
  • App preferences and settings stored locally on your device

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Calculate daily calorie, macro, and micronutrient targets
  • Generate personalized workout plans and nutrition insights using AI
  • Provide AI-powered coaching evaluations and food analysis
  • Track progress, weight trends, and goal projections over time
  • Sync data securely across your devices via cloud storage
  • Process food images and menu photos for nutrition estimation
  • Manage your premium subscription status
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs through crash reporting

4. Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Your fitness data (food logs, weight history, workout plans, and account settings) is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, linked to your authenticated account
  • Local Storage: App preferences, cached search results, and offline data are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore and Room database
  • Data Sync: Data is synchronized between your local device and cloud storage to enable cross-device access

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. Matthews Fitness uses the following trusted third-party services to operate core functionality:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication (sign-in), secure cloud data storage (Firestore), Cloud Functions for server-side AI processing, and crash reporting
  • Google Gemini API — AI-powered nutrition analysis, food photo recognition, meal decomposition, workout plan generation, recipe creation, and coaching evaluations
  • OpenFoodFacts — Barcode-based nutrition data lookup (open-source food database)
  • Google Play Billing — Subscription and in-app purchase management

These services are used strictly to support app functionality and user experience. Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy.

6. Permissions

Matthews Fitness requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera — Used for barcode scanning and food/menu photo capture. Camera access is optional and only activated when you use scanning features
  • Internet — Required for cloud sync, AI features, barcode lookup, and authentication

Matthews Fitness does not access your location, contacts, microphone, or phone storage beyond what is needed for app functionality.

7. Health Disclaimer

Matthews Fitness provides general health and fitness information for educational and informational purposes only. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment and is not a substitute for professional medical care.

AI-generated content — including nutrition estimates, coaching evaluations, workout suggestions, and meal plans — may not be 100% accurate. Always verify information from official sources when accuracy is critical.

Before starting any diet, exercise, or nutrition program, consult with a qualified healthcare provider, especially if you have medical conditions, take medications, or have specific dietary requirements.

8. Estimates & Results Disclaimer

Nutrition values, calorie calculations, macro targets, activity guidance, and projected goal timelines are estimates based on user-provided information, AI analysis, and available data sources. Individual results may vary, and outcomes are not guaranteed.

By using this app, you acknowledge that you are solely responsible for your health decisions. The developers are not liable for any health issues, injuries, or adverse effects resulting from use of this app.

9. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain user data for as long as an account remains active. You may request deletion of your Matthews Fitness account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  • In-app: Go to Settings → About Us → Delete Account
  • By email: Send a request to MatthewsFitnessHelp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your Matthews Fitness Account ID (Support ID), which can be found in the app under Settings → About Us
  • If available, you may also include the email address associated with your account

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Upon deletion, your data is removed from both cloud storage and our systems. Certain data may be retained temporarily where required for legal, security, or operational purposes.

Matthews Fitness does not sell user data.

10. Children's Privacy

Matthews Fitness is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that data has been collected from a user under 18, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
