Matthews Fitness – Account Deletion

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Matthews Fitness and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Tap Settings (gear icon).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm when prompted. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

What gets deleted

Your profile, food logs, workout history, weight entries, meal plans, recipes, preferences, and any cloud-synced data are permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to log in or access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly:

matthewsfitnesshelp@gmail.com

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and delete all data within 7 business days.
